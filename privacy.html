Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What is collected

When you use the contact form on this site, your name, email address, and message are collected via Formspree. No other personal data is collected automatically. This site does not use tracking pixels, analytics software, or advertising cookies.

Why it is collected

Contact form submissions are used solely to respond to your transmission. Your information is not sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing without your consent.

How long it is kept

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond and resolve the inquiry. They are not stored in a database or CRM.

Third-party services

Contact form submissions are processed by Formspree. Purchases are made through Etsy and are governed by Etsy's privacy policy. Bleak Pattern Co. does not store payment information. Fonts and static assets are served via jsDelivr CDN.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information submitted via the contact form. Send a transmission to request this.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to Bleak Pattern Co. via the contact form on the main site.
